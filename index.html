Criticism
will continue
TO be
directed inward

  • Who are the critics?
  • How did they become a player?
  • Why are they relevant?
  • Are the critics knowledgeable?

Spectators have been in the picture for so long, but there was no platform for them to express themselves. However, with the rapid development of the internet, they now have a variety of social media platforms on which to voice their opinions.

Views expressed by spectators may be mistaken for either love or hatred. Does the fact that they have a single voice qualify them as knowledgeable? The three articles below will give you a detailed explanation of why spectators, or loyal customers, behave the way they do.
